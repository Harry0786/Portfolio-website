ISF App

Version 1.0.0

About ISF App

The IETE Student Forum (ISF) App is a comprehensive platform designed to streamline and enhance the management of student forum activities within educational institutions. It serves as a central hub for coordinating events, meetings, and member activities, making it easier for student leaders and faculty advisors to maintain effective communication and organization within the forum.

Built with modern Android technologies, the ISF App revolutionizes how student forums operate by digitizing traditional management processes. From tracking attendance at events to managing forum inventory, the app provides a seamless experience for both organizers and members. It eliminates the need for multiple communication channels and paper-based systems, bringing everything together in one efficient, user-friendly platform.

Key Features

Event Management with RSVP System
Meeting Creation and Scheduling
Interactive Members Directory
Inventory Management System
Digital Attendance Tracking
Analytics and Reports
